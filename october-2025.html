GreenSEO Event - October 2025

At the brightonSEO fringe's fourth GreenSEO event, we learnt about the Clean Creatives movement, how to migrate websites sustainably and how to spread the green message through behavioural change.

Introduction: Stuart Davies: The GreenSEO movement and its mission

Inspirational: Alex Holliman: Clean Creatives: How creatives and agencies are pledging to decline future contracts with the fossil fuel industry.

Practical: Dave Cousin: Upcoming website migration? Here’s how to do it sustainably.

Behavioural Change: Heidi Swigon: Here, there, everywhere: How to spread the green message.

Time: Wed 22 Oct, 2025 | 18:30 – 20:30
